FAQ

Questions people ask before starting.

A few quick answers for students joining YoungMinds and businesses planning a project with us.

How long does it take to get a response after I apply or hire you?
We usually respond on WhatsApp within 24 hours. For client projects, that first reply includes next steps and a rough plan. For member applications, we confirm your details and review fit before moving ahead.
Do I need prior experience to join YoungMinds?
No. Beginners can still apply if they show commitment, strong communication, and a willingness to learn. If you already have portfolio work, it helps, but motivation and consistency matter a lot too.
What services can I hire your team for?
You can hire us for websites, graphic design, content writing, AI solutions, social media support, and video editing. If your work spans multiple areas, use the hire form and choose the option that best matches your needs.
How does pricing and payment work for client projects?
We collect your budget during the form, then share a quote after reviewing the scope. Most projects begin with a 50% advance, and the remaining payment is handled based on the agreed delivery milestones.
Can I update my details after submitting a form?
Yes. If something changes after submission, just reply when our team contacts you and we can update your project or application details before the next stage starts.
